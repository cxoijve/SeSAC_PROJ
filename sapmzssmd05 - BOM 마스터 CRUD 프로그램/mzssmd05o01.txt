*&---------------------------------------------------------------------*
*       text
*----------------------------------------------------------------------*
MODULE modify_screen_0200 OUTPUT.

  LOOP AT SCREEN.
    " gt_info_200R에 아이템이 있으면 변경할 수 없도록 꺼버려야 함.
    IF screen-group1 = 'CND'.
      IF gt_info_0200r IS NOT INITIAL.
        screen-input = 0.
      ELSE.
        screen-input = 1.
      ENDIF.
    ENDIF.
    MODIFY SCREEN.
  ENDLOOP.
ENDMODULE.
